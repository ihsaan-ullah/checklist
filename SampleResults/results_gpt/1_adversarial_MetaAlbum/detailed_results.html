Supplemental Details: While the main paper should include all critical experimental details to understand the results, additional information such as extensive hyperparameter searches, ablation studies, or any data preprocessing steps can be moved to supplemental material if space is a constraint. Versioning of Datasets and Code: Indicate the version of any external datasets used, and provide access or reference to the exact version of your own dataset (Meta-Album) used in the experiments.
