  • Privacy and Fairness: The paper does not address potential limitations or concerns related to privacy and fairness, which are relevant considerations for image classification tasks.

Recommendations for Improvement

  • Dedicated Limitations Section: Create a separate section titled "Limitations" to comprehensively discuss the limitations of the work.
  • Analyze Assumptions: Clearly state the key assumptions underlying the methods and analyze how violations of those assumptions could impact the results.
  • Acknowledge Scope: Explicitly mention the limited scope of the current experiments and avoid overgeneralization of the findings.
  • Discuss Influencing Factors: Analyze how data bias, image quality, and other factors affect the performance of different algorithms.
